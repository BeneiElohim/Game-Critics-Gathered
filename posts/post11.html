Review

Elden Ring is FromSoftware's Magnum Opus. It is the culmination of everything they have learned from their previous games. It is the game that will define souls-like genre. The developers seem to have taken the concept of open world that has been used very extensively and diluted by extension of it to the point of being meaningless. They have taken the concept of open world and made it meaningful again.
