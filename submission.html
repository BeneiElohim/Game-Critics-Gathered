So, you would like to submit to GCG? This is the page for you!

Before you submit, please read the following:

  • Be respectful

    We are a community of people who love games and want to share their opinions with others. We are not here to attack people, but to discuss games. Please be respectful to others and their opinions. Any submission that is not respectful will be rejected.

  • Be original

    We want to hear your opinions on games, not what someone else has already said. Please do not copy and paste other people's reviews. Any submission that is not original will be rejected.

  • Be honest

    We want to hear your honest opinions on games. For this reason, we do not allow any submissions that are fake or exaggerated. Any free codes for games has to be disclosed. Any submission that is not honest will be rejected.

  • Be mindfull of the guidelines

    We have a set of guidelines that you must follow when submitting to GCG. Please read them carefully before submitting. Any submission that does not follow the guidelines will be rejected.

Step 1: Download the post-template.html

Go to the bottom of this page and download the HTML file given to you.
It is very important that you DO NOT change anything in the file other than what is specified.
If you do, your submission will be rejected.

Step 2: Open the HTML.

Open the HTML file in a text editor.

Step 3: Download the HTML.

Fill in the information in the HTML file.
The information you need to fill in is specified in the file.
Only the comments are needed to be filled in. If you do not fill in the information, your submission will be rejected.

Step 4: Select and add images.

You need to add 5 images to your submission.
The images must be in the images folder.
The images must be in the format of .jpg or .png.
The first image is your cover image.

Step 5: Send your file.

Mail your submission to imt7@student.london.ac.uk Our team will get back to you and insert your page into the blog as soon as possible.
