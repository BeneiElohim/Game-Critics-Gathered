Frequently Asked Questions

You'll find answers to some of your most emerging questions here.

  • How does your website approach game criticism differently than other websites?

    We believe that criticism should be to the point and work for its purpose, which is to give the player an opinion on purchasing the game. We also believe that true critics are players. We are not journalists, we are not developers, we are not publishers. We are players.

  • How can I submit my game review to your website?

    You can go to the submission page and get the full information from there.

  • How long does it typically take for a submitted review to be published on your website?

    It can take up to 3 days. We are a small team and we are trying to do our best to publish all the reviews as soon as possible.

  • Are there any specific types of games or genres that your website is focused on?

    No. We try to stick to the most popular games and genres, but we are open to any game that is worth reviewing.

  • How do you ensure that reviews on your website are fair and unbiased?

    We don't!. Our users do. We are not trying to moderate the reviews. We are just trying to give the players a platform to share their opinions.

  • Can I submit a review of a game that I received for free from a developer or publisher?

    Yes, you can. However this has to be stated in the review.

  • Will my name be displayed publicly alongside my review?

    You can choose to display your name or not. It is up to you. Just let us know!

  • Can I edit or update my review after it has been published?

    Nope, once its out, it is out.

  • How can I get in touch with your website's team if I have questions or concerns?

    You can send a mail to our team at imt7@student.london.ac.uk and we will get back to you as soon as possible.
